BENEDICTA OPOKU-MENSAH

Biography

Benedicta Opoku-Mensah is an artist and educator originally from Ghana, currently residing in Gainesville, Florida. In 2018, she graduated from Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology in Kumasi, Ghana, with a Bachelor of Arts in Integrated Rural Art and Industry. Before pursuing her Master of Fine Arts (MFA) at the University of Florida, she served as a teaching and research assistant at the same institution where she had completed her undergraduate studies. Benedicta has been awarded the prestigious University of Florida's Grinter Fellowship for the years 2023 to 2025.
